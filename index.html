Revolution Multiplier: 

A - Increases the revolution speed of all planets by 1 unit

D - Decreases the revolution speed of all planets by 1 unit

W - Rotate the axes 0.01 units

S - Rotate the axes -0.01 units

Shift + W/S - Rotates the axes 1 unit

L Click - Camera orbits

R Click - Camera moves

Scroll - Camera zooms
